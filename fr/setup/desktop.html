Stream TVMaster on Windows & Mac

Whether you’re multitasking at a desk or piping HDMI to a projector, this guide shows how to watch TVMaster via VLC media player and Kodi on Ordinateur de Bureau. You’ll learn playlist import, Guide TV configuration, DVR recording, and picture optimisation for every league.

Platforms: Windows 10/11, macOS 12+, Linux (VLC/Kodi) Installation time: 6 minutes · Skill: Beginner Exigences: VLC or Kodi installed, TVMaster playlist & Guide TV URLs

Why Ordinateur de Bureau streaming matters

  • Analysts can watch multiple fixtures simultaneously with VLC’s multi-window support.
  • Creatives record broadcast-grade clips for highlights (respect rights) using VLC and OBS.
  • Kodi enables remote-friendly navigation for HTPC setups with Xbox or PS controllers.
  • HDMI connects PCs to big screens when TV Intelligentes don’t support sideloading.

Watch via VLC (fastest method)

  1. Installer VLC – Télécharger from videolan.org and Installer on Windows or Mac.
  2. Open network stream – Launch VLC, go to Media ▸ Open Network Stream.
  3. Paste the playlist – Enter the TVMaster M3U URL and click Play. VLC loads the first channel instantly.
  4. Browse chaînes – Press Ctrl+L (Cmd+L on Mac) to open the channel playlist. Double-click to switch between Sky Sports, beIN, NBC, etc.
  5. Record highlights – Enable View ▸ Advanced Controls, then press the red record button to capture clips locally.

Alternate: Kodi + PVR IPTV Simple Client

Enable add-on: In Kodi, go to Add-ons ▸ My add-ons ▸ PVR Clients, enable PVR IPTV Simple Client.

Configurer: Set Location ▸ Remote path, paste the TVMaster M3U URL and XMLTV Guide TV URL, then restart Kodi.

Big-screen mode: Kodi’s TV section pulls channel logos, Guide TV grids, and catch-up tags—perfect for living-room HTPC lovers.

Optimisation & troubleshooting

Audio delay? In VLC, use Tools ▸ Track Synchronisation to nudge audio ±0.1s until commentary lines up.

Choppy playback? Switch to Tools ▸ Preferences ▸ Input/Codecs ▸ Hardware-accelerated decoding ▸ Automatic, and raise network caching to 1500 ms.

Playlist not updating? Clear VLC cache (delete ml.xspf), or in Kodi hit TV ▸ Guide ▸ Clear data after renewing your abonnement.
