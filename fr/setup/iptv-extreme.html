1. Introduction to IPTV Extreme

What is IPTV Extreme?

IPTV Extreme is a powerful and feature-rich IPTV player application designed for Android devices. It allows you to watch Chaînes TV en Direct, Films, TV series, and VOD content using your IPTV abonnement. The app is known for its user-friendly interface, extensive format support, and Fonctionnalités Avancées like recording and catch-up TV.

Note: IPTV Extreme is a player application only. You need an active IPTV abonnement (like TVMaster VIP) to watch content.

Key Fonctionnalités

Live TV & VOD

Watch Chaînes en Direct, Films, and TV series with smooth playback

Guide TV Support

Electronic Program Guide with full TV schedules

Catch-up TV

Watch missed programs from the past 7 jours

Recording

Record your favorite shows and Films

Favorites

Create custom lists of your favorite chaînes

Parental Control

Lock chaînes and content with PIN protection

Compatible Devices

  • Android Phones & Tablets - Android 5.0 and above
  • Android Boîtier TVes - Tout Android TV devices
  • Amazon Fire TV Stick - Tout Fire TV models
  • TV Intelligentes - Android TV powered televisions

2. Guide d'Installation

2.1

Android Phone/Tablette Installation

  1. Open Google Play Store

    Tap the Play Store icon on your Android device.

  2. Rechercher for IPTV Extreme

    Type "IPTV Extreme" in the Rechercher bar and press Rechercher.

  3. Select the App

    Look for "IPTV Extreme Pro" by Paolo Federico (or the free version "IPTV Extreme").

  4. Installer

    Tap the "Installer" button and wait for the Télécharger to complete.

  5. Open the App

    Once installed, tap "Open" to launch IPTV Extreme.

2.2

Amazon Fire TV Stick Installation

  1. Enable Apps from Unknown Sources

    Go to Settings → My Fire TV → Developer Options → Installer Unknown Apps → Turn on for your browser.

  2. Télécharger Downloader App

    From Amazon Appstore, Rechercher and Installer "Downloader" by AFTVnews.com.

  3. Open Downloader

    Launch the Downloader app and accept permissions.

  4. Enter URL

    In the URL field, enter: https://www.iptvextreme.eu/ and Télécharger the APK.

  5. Installer APK

    Once downloaded, click "Installer" and wait for installation to complete.

  6. Launch App

    You can now find IPTV Extreme in your Fire TV apps section.

Alternative: You can also sideload IPTV Extreme using Apps2Fire or similar tools from your Android phone.
2.3

Android TV Installation

  1. Open Google Play Store

    Navigate to the Play Store on your Android TV.

  2. Rechercher for IPTV Extreme

    Use the Rechercher function or voice Rechercher to find "IPTV Extreme".

  3. Installer

    Select the app and click "Installer".

  4. Launch

    Open the app from your Android TV Accueil screen or app drawer.

3. First Launch & Initial Installation

When you first open IPTV Extreme, you'll be greeted with a Installation wizard to help you Commencer.

Initial Configuration Steps

  1. Welcome Screen

    Read the welcome message and tap "Next" or "Commencer".

  2. Grant Permissions

    The app may request storage permissions for recording and Guide TV data. Tap "Allow" to grant access.

  3. Select Language

    Choose your preferred language from the list. You can change this later in settings.

  4. Choose Player

    IPTV Extreme offers built-in player or external player support. For best experience, select "Internal Player".

  5. Ready to Add Playlist

    You'll now see the main screen with an option to add your IPTV playlist.

Tip: The Installation wizard only appears on first launch. Tout settings can be changed later from the app's settings menu.

4. Adding Your IPTV Playlist

IPTV Extreme supports two main methods for adding playlists: M3U URL and Xtream Codes API. Use the credentials provided by your TVMaster VIP abonnement.

4.1

Method 1: Add M3U URL Playlist

  1. Open Playlist Menu

    Tap the menu icon (☰) in the top-left corner, then select "Playlists".

  2. Add Nouveau Playlist

    Tap the "+" button or "Add Playlist" option.

  3. Select "Remote Playlist (URL)"

    Choose the remote playlist option to add an M3U URL.

  4. Enter Playlist Details

    Fill in the following information:

    Playlist Name: TVMaster VIP Playlist URL: http://your-server.com:port/get.php?username=your_username&password=your_password&type=m3u_plus&output=ts Replace with your actual credentials from TVMaster VIP
  5. Économisez and Load

    Tap "Économisez" or "OK". The app will Télécharger and parse your playlist. This may take 30-60 seconds.

  6. Verify chaînes

    Once loaded, you should see Tout your chaînes categorized in the channel list.

4.2

Method 2: Add Xtream Codes API

  1. Open Playlist Menu

    Tap the menu icon (☰) and select "Playlists".

  2. Add Nouveau Playlist

    Tap the "+" button or "Add Playlist".

  3. Select "Xtream Codes"

    Choose the Xtream Codes API option (recommended for best performance).

  4. Enter API Credentials

    Fill in your TVMaster VIP credentials:

    Playlist Name: TVMaster VIP Server URL: http://your-server.com:port Username: your_username Password: your_password Use credentials provided in your TVMaster VIP welcome email
  5. Économisez and Connect

    Tap "Économisez" or "Connect". The app will authenticate and load your content.

  6. Browse Content

    You'll now have access to Live TV, Films, and Series sections.

Recommendation: Xtream Codes API is preferred as it provides better organization, faster Chargement, and access to VOD categories, series with episode lists, and catch-up TV Fonctionnalités.

6. Guide TV (Electronic Program Guide) Installation

Guide TV provides TV listings showing what's currently playing and upcoming programs. IPTV Extreme supports automatic Guide TV from Xtream Codes or manual Guide TV URLs.

6.1

Automatic Guide TV (Xtream Codes)

If you're using Xtream Codes API, Guide TV is automatically included:

  1. Guide TV Auto-loads

    Guide TV data is downloaded automatically when you add a Xtream Codes playlist.

  2. Auto-Update

    Guide TV refreshes automatically every 24 hours.

  3. Verify Guide TV

    Open any channel - you should see current program name and time below channel logo.

6.2

Manual Guide TV Installation (M3U Playlists)

For M3U playlists, you may need to add Guide TV URL manually:

  1. Get Guide TV URL

    Obtain the Guide TV URL from your TVMaster VIP account or welcome email.

  2. Open Settings

    Go to Menu (☰) → Settings → Guide TV.

  3. Add Guide TV Source

    Tap "Add Guide TV Source" or "Guide TV URL".

  4. Enter URL
    Guide TV URL: http://your-server.com:port/xmltv.php?username=your_username&password=your_password Replace with your TVMaster VIP credentials
  5. Télécharger Guide TV

    Tap "Télécharger Guide TV" or "Update Guide TV". First Télécharger may take 2-5 minutes.

  6. Enable Auto-Update

    Turn on "Auto-update Guide TV" to refresh daily at a specified time.

Guide TV Update Schedule: Set Guide TV auto-update during low-usage hours (e.g., 3:00 AM) to avoid interrupting viewing. Guide TV data is typically 5-10 MB per jour.

7. Fonctionnalités Avancées

Managing Favorites

Create custom lists of your most-watched chaînes:

  1. Add to Favorites

    Long-press any channel in the list and select "Add to Favorites" or tap the star icon.

  2. Access Favorites

    Go to Menu (☰) → Favorites to see Tout your favorite chaînes in one place.

  3. Remove from Favorites

    Long-press a favorite channel and select "Remove from Favorites".

  4. Reorder Favorites

    Long-press and drag to reorder chaînes in your favorites list.

Parental Control

Lock specific chaînes or categories to restrict access:

  1. Enable Parental Control

    Go to Settings → Parental Control and toggle it on.

  2. Set PIN Code

    Create a 4-digit PIN code. Remember this - it's required to unlock content.

  3. Lock chaînes/Categories

    Long-press any channel or category and select "Lock". Locked content shows a padlock icon.

  4. Unlock Content

    Attempting to access locked content prompts for PIN entry.

Catch-up TV

Watch programs from the past 7 jours (requires Xtream Codes and fournisseur support):

  1. Open Guide TV

    While watching a channel, tap the Info button to open Guide TV.

  2. Select Past Program

    Browse the timeline and tap any program from the past 7 jours.

  3. Start Playback

    The program will load and start playing from the beginning.

  4. Player Controls

    Use pause, play, and seek controls just like VOD content.

Note: Catch-up availability depends on your IPTV fournisseur. TVMaster VIP offers catch-up on most major chaînes.

Recording Programs

Record live TV to watch later (storage permission required):

  1. Start Recording

    While watching live TV, tap the record button (red circle icon).

  2. Choose Duration

    Select recording duration or choose "Until manually stopped".

  3. Recording Active

    A notification appears confirming recording. You can minimize and use other apps.

  4. Access Recordings

    Go to Menu (☰) → Recordings to Tout Voir saved content.

  5. Manage Storage

    Recordings are saved to internal storage. Delete old recordings to free up space.

Rechercher Functionality

Quickly find chaînes, Films, or series:

  1. Open Rechercher

    Tap the Rechercher icon (magnifying glass) in the top toolbar.

  2. Enter Query

    Type channel name, movie title, or series name.

  3. Filter Results

    Results show across Tout content types (Live TV, Films, Series).

  4. Quick Access

    Tap any result to immediately start playback.

9. Troubleshooting Common Issues

chaînes Not Chargement / Buffering

Causes:

  • Slow internet connection
  • Server overload
  • Incorrect buffer settings

Solutions:

  • Check internet speed (minimum 10 Mbps recommended)
  • Increase buffer size: Settings → Player → Buffer Size → 10 seconds
  • Enable hardware acceleration
  • Switch to lower quality stream if available
  • Restart router and device

Black Screen / No Video

Causes:

  • Codec incompatibility
  • Hardware acceleration issues
  • Corrupt stream

Solutions:

  • Go to Settings → Player → Video Decoder → Switch to Software
  • Toggle hardware acceleration off/on
  • Try different channel to verify if issue is stream-specific
  • Update IPTV Extreme to latest version
  • Clear app cache: Settings → Storage → Clear Cache

No Audio / Audio Sync Issues

Causes:

  • Audio codec not supported
  • Audio delay settings
  • Device audio output issue

Solutions:

  • Check device volume and ensure not muted
  • Settings → Player → Audio Output → Try different option
  • Adjust audio delay: Long-press during playback → Audio Delay
  • Switch audio track if multiple available
  • Restart the app

Guide TV Not Showing / Not Updating

Causes:

  • Guide TV URL not configured
  • Guide TV Télécharger failed
  • Auto-update disabled

Solutions:

  • Verify Guide TV URL in Settings → Guide TV
  • Manually trigger Guide TV update: Settings → Guide TV → Update Now
  • Enable auto-update: Settings → Guide TV → Auto-update Guide TV
  • Check storage space (Guide TV requires 5-10 MB)
  • Re-add playlist if using Xtream Codes

Playlist Not Chargement

Causes:

  • Incorrect URL or credentials
  • Server down or maintenance
  • abonnement expired

Solutions:

  • Double-check credentials match your TVMaster VIP account
  • Verify URL format (include http:// or https://)
  • Test credentials in browser or another device
  • Check abonnement status in TVMaster VIP dashboard
  • Contact TVMaster VIP support if issue persists

App Crashes / Freezes

Causes:

  • Insufficient device memory
  • App data corruption
  • Outdated app version

Solutions:

  • Clear app cache and data: Device Settings → Apps → IPTV Extreme
  • Close background apps to free up RAM
  • Update to latest IPTV Extreme version
  • Restart device
  • Reinstall app if problems continue

10. Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use IPTV Extreme on multiple devices?

Yes! You can Installer IPTV Extreme on unlimited devices. However, simultaneous streaming is limited by your TVMaster VIP abonnement plan. Most plans allow 1-3 concurrent connections. Check your abonnement details for specific limits.

Is IPTV Extreme free or paid?

IPTV Extreme offers both a free version with ads and a Pro version ($5.99 one-time purchase) without ads and with additional Fonctionnalités like custom themes and advanced recording options. Both versions work with your TVMaster VIP abonnement.

How much internet speed do I need?

Recommended speeds: SD quality (480p): 5 Mbps | Qualité HD (720p): 10 Mbps | Full HD (1080p): 20 Mbps | 4K Ultra HD: 50 Mbps. These are per stream, so multiple devices require proportionally Plus bandwidth.

Why do some chaînes buffer Plus than others?

Different chaînes have different bitrates (video quality). Sports and movie chaînes in HD/4K require Plus bandwidth. Peak viewing times can also cause congestion. Try increasing buffer size or watching during off-peak hours for smoother playback.

Can I use VPN with IPTV Extreme?

Yes, IPTV Extreme works with VPN services. A VPN can improve privacy and may help access geo-restricted content. However, VPNs can sometimes slow down streaming. Choose a fast VPN server close to your location or the IPTV server location for best results.

How do I backup my settings and favorites?

Go to Settings → Backup & Restore → Create Backup. This saves your playlists, favorites, Guide TV settings, and app preferences to a file. You can restore this backup on another device or after reinstalling the app.

What's the difference between M3U and Xtream Codes?

M3U: Simple playlist format, single URL, Tout content in one list. Xtream Codes: Advanced API with better organization, separate sections for Live TV/Films/Series, faster Chargement, automatic Guide TV, catch-up support. We recommend Xtream Codes for best experience.

Where are recordings saved and how much space do they use?

Recordings are saved to your device's internal storage by default (you can change this in Settings → Storage → Recording Location). Space usage depends on quality and duration: SD quality uses ~1 GB/hour, HD uses ~2-3 GB/hour, Full HD uses ~4-5 GB/hour.
