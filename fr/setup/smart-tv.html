Stream TVMaster on Samsung & LG TV Intelligentes

Use Smart IPTV or DuplexPlay to bring TVMaster’s full IPTV catalogue to Samsung Tizen and LG webOS televisions. This guide covers MAC activation, playlist upload, channel logo syncing, parental locks, and buffering fixes—no extra hardware required.

Compatible with 2016+ Samsung Tizen & LG webOS TV Intelligentes Installation time: 12 minutes · Skill: Intermediate Exigences: MAC address, TVMaster playlist URL, €5.49 Smart IPTV license

Smart IPTV set-up (recommended)

  1. Installer Smart IPTV – Open the Samsung or LG content store, Rechercher for Smart IPTV, and Installer the Essai Gratuit app.
  2. Note your MAC address – Launch the app to display the TV MAC (e.g., 54:88:0E:xx:xx). Keep the splash screen open.
  3. Upload the TVMaster playlist – Visit siptv.app/mylist, enter the MAC, paste the TVMaster M3U URL plus Guide TV link, tick Économisez Online, and click Send.
  4. Activer the app – Purchase the one-time €5.49 license at siptv.app/activation, using the same MAC address.
  5. Reload the playlist – Reopen Smart IPTV on the TV, press the red button (or hold OK) to refresh chaînes, categories, and program guide.
  6. Customise display – Go to Settings ▸ General to enable Logos from Guide TV, set Timeshift Buffer to 2 hours, and apply parental PIN for adult categories.

DuplexPlay alternative (if Smart IPTV unavailable)

Installation: Télécharger DuplexPlay from the store, note the device ID and key, then Activer via duplexplay.com/Activer.

Playlist load: Use the Upload Playlist form to add TVMaster’s M3U URL and assign region filters (UK, US, LatAm, MENA) for faster navigation.

Advantages: Supports multi-audio switching, trailers inside VOD, and remote-friendly grid view ideal for Sports bars and hospitality clients.

Troubleshooting

No chaînes after upload? Verify you pasted the M3U URL correctly, disable MAC filtering on your router, and ensure the TV is hardwired or on 5 GHz Wi-Fi.

Stretched aspect ratio? In Smart IPTV, set Aspect Ratio ▸ Screen Fit or use the remote’s P.Size button on Samsung to cycle through modes.

App removed from Samsung store? Side-load the Tizen package via USB (Télécharger from siptv.app) or direct customers to DuplexPlay / Net IPTV.
