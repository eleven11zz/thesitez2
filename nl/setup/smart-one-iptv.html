1. What is SMART ONE IPTV?

About SMART ONE IPTV

SMART ONE IPTV is a powerful and user-friendly IPTV player application that allows you to stream live TV channels, movies, and series from your IPTV service provider. It supports M3U playlists, Xtream Codes API, and offers advanced features like EPG (Electronic Program Guide), catch-up TV, recording, and parental controls.

Key Features

  • Support for M3U & Xtream Codes
  • EPG / TV Guide integration
  • Catch-up TV (7-14 days)
  • Favorites & channel groups
  • Parental control (PIN lock)
  • Multi-language support
  • 4K & HD streaming
  • Built-in video player

Compatible Devices

  • Android phones & tablets (5.0+)
  • Android TV (7.0+)
  • Amazon Fire TV & Fire Stick
  • NVIDIA Shield TV
  • Mi Box, Xiaomi TV
  • Samsung, LG Smart TVs (Android)
  • Generic Android TV Boxes

4. Adding Your IPTV Playlist

SMART ONE IPTV supports two main methods to add your IPTV service: M3U URL and Xtream Codes API. Choose the method provided by TVMaster VIP in your welcome email.

Method 1

Adding M3U Playlist URL

  1. Tap "Add Playlist"

    On the main screen, tap the "+ Add Playlist" button.

  2. Select "M3U URL"

    Choose "M3U URL" from the playlist type options.

  3. Enter Playlist Name

    Give your playlist a name (e.g., "TVMaster VIP English").

  4. Paste M3U URL

    Paste the M3U playlist URL provided in your TVMaster welcome email.

    http://your-server.com/get.php?username=YOUR_USERNAME&password=YOUR_PASSWORD&type=m3u_plus
  5. Optional: Add EPG URL

    If you have an EPG (TV Guide) URL, paste it in the EPG URL field.

  6. Save Playlist

    Tap "Save" or "Add Playlist" button.

  7. Wait for Loading

    The app will download and parse your playlist (may take 1-3 minutes for large playlists).

Tip: You can add multiple playlists (different regions or services) by repeating this process.

Method 2

Adding Xtream Codes API

  1. Tap "Add Playlist"

    On the main screen, tap the "+ Add Playlist" button.

  2. Select "Xtream Codes API"

    Choose "Xtream Codes" or "API Login" from the options.

  3. Enter Connection Details

    Fill in the following fields from your TVMaster welcome email:

    • Server URL: http://your-server.com:port
    • Username: Your assigned username
    • Password: Your assigned password
  4. Save and Connect

    Tap "Login" or "Connect" to authenticate with the server.

  5. Categories Will Load Automatically

    The app will fetch all channels, VOD, and series categories automatically.

Recommended: Xtream Codes API provides automatic EPG updates and better organization than M3U URLs.

6. Setting Up EPG (TV Guide)

6.1

Automatic EPG (Xtream Codes)

If you added your playlist using Xtream Codes API, EPG is automatically configured. No additional setup needed!

EPG will update automatically every 24 hours.

6.2

Manual EPG Setup (M3U Users)

  1. Open Settings

    Go to Menu ‚Üí Settings ‚Üí EPG Settings.

  2. Enter EPG URL

    Paste the EPG XML URL provided by TVMaster VIP.

    http://your-server.com/xmltv.php?username=YOUR_USERNAME&password=YOUR_PASSWORD
  3. Set Update Frequency

    Choose how often to update EPG (recommended: every 12 hours).

  4. Save and Update

    Tap "Save" then "Update EPG Now" to load program data.

6.3

Using the TV Guide

  • View current and upcoming programs for all channels
  • Set reminders for shows (notification before start)
  • Browse program details (description, duration, cast)
  • Record shows (if catch-up TV is available)
  • Navigate by time slots or channel grid

  1. Add to Favorites

9. Probleemoplossing Guide

Buffering / Freezing Issues

Solutions:
  1. Check your internet speed (minimum 10 Mbps for HD, 25 Mbps for 4K)
  2. Go to Settings ‚Üí Player ‚Üí increase Buffer Size to "Large"
  3. Switch Video Decoder to "Hardware" if currently on Software
  4. Restart your router/modem
  5. Close background apps consuming bandwidth
  6. Try lower quality streams if available

Channels Not Loading

Solutions:
  1. Check if your subscription is active (contact TVMaster support)
  2. Verify M3U URL or Xtream Codes credentials are correct
  3. Go to Settings ‚Üí Playlist ‚Üí "Refresh Playlist"
  4. Delete and re-add the playlist
  5. Check firewall/VPN is not blocking connection

No EPG / TV Guide Data

Solutions:
  1. Verify EPG URL is correctly entered in settings
  2. Go to Settings ‚Üí EPG ‚Üí "Update EPG Now"
  3. Wait 5-10 minutes for EPG data to download
  4. Check internet connection during EPG update
  5. Ensure sufficient storage space (minimum 500MB)

No Audio / Black Screen

Solutions:
  1. Check device volume is not muted
  2. Try switching Audio Track (Menu ‚Üí Audio ‚Üí Select Track)
  3. Change Video Decoder to "Software" in settings
  4. Update SMART ONE IPTV to latest version
  5. Test with different channel to isolate issue

App Crashes / Force Close

Solutions:
  1. Clear app cache: Settings ‚Üí Apps ‚Üí SMART ONE ‚Üí Clear Cache
  2. Clear app data (warning: will delete playlists)
  3. Restart device completely
  4. Uninstall and reinstall SMART ONE IPTV
  5. Check for app updates in Play Store

Forgot Parental Control PIN

Solutions:
  1. Default PIN is usually: 0000 or 1234
  2. Go to device Settings ‚Üí Apps ‚Üí SMART ONE ‚Üí Clear Data (resets PIN)
  3. Warning: Clearing data will remove all playlists and settings
  4. After reset, you'll need to re-add playlists

10. Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use SMART ONE IPTV on multiple devices?

Yes! You can install SMART ONE IPTV on all your devices. However, the number of simultaneous streams depends on your TVMaster VIP subscription plan. Most plans allow 1-3 concurrent streams.

Does SMART ONE IPTV work with VPN?

Yes, SMART ONE IPTV works perfectly with VPN services. If you're experiencing geo-restrictions or want enhanced privacy, you can use any VPN app before launching SMART ONE IPTV.

How do I update my playlist when channels change?

Go to Settings ‚Üí Playlist Manager ‚Üí Select your playlist ‚Üí Tap "Refresh" or "Update Playlist". This will fetch the latest channel list from your IPTV provider.

Can I record live TV shows?

Recording functionality depends on your IPTV service and device capabilities. Some providers offer cloud DVR through catch-up TV features. Check with TVMaster VIP support for recording options on your plan.

What internet speed do I need for HD and 4K streaming?

How do I backup my favorites and settings?

Go to Settings ‚Üí Backup & Restore ‚Üí Create Backup. The app will save your playlists, favorites, and settings to a file. You can restore this on a new device or after reinstalling.

Why do some channels show "No Stream Available"?

This usually means the channel is temporarily offline, experiencing technical issues, or not included in your subscription package. Contact TVMaster VIP support if the issue persists across multiple channels.

Can I customize the channel order?

Yes! Go to Settings ‚Üí Channel Manager. You can reorder channels by dragging, create custom categories, hide channels you don't watch, and organize everything to your preference.
