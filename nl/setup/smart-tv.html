Stream TVMaster on Samsung & LG Smart TVs

Use Smart IPTV or DuplexPlay to bring TVMaster’s full IPTV catalogue to Samsung Tizen and LG webOS televisions. This guide covers MAC activation, playlist upload, channel logo syncing, parental locks, and buffering fixes—no extra hardware required.

Compatible with 2016+ Samsung Tizen & LG webOS Smart TVs Setup time: 12 minutes · Skill: Intermediate Requirements: MAC address, TVMaster playlist URL, €5.49 Smart IPTV license

Owners love the simplicity of running TVMaster directly on their TV OS. Highlight these benefits during onboarding emails and live chat:

Smart IPTV set-up (recommended)

  1. Installeer Smart IPTV – Open the Samsung or LG content store, search for Smart IPTV, and install the free trial app.
  2. Note your MAC address – Launch the app to display the TV MAC (e.g., 54:88:0E:xx:xx). Keep the splash screen open.
  3. Upload the TVMaster playlist – Visit siptv.app/mylist, enter the MAC, paste the TVMaster M3U URL plus EPG link, tick Save Online, and click Send.
  4. Activate the app – Purchase the one-time €5.49 license at siptv.app/activation, using the same MAC address.
  5. Reload the playlist – Reopen Smart IPTV on the TV, press the red button (or hold OK) to refresh channels, categories, and program guide.
  6. Customise display – Go to Settings ▸ General to enable Logos from EPG, set Timeshift Buffer to 2 hours, and apply parental PIN for adult categories.

DuplexPlay alternative (if Smart IPTV unavailable)

Installeeratie: Download DuplexPlay from the store, note the device ID and key, then activate via duplexplay.com/activate.

Playlist load: Use the Upload Playlist form to add TVMaster’s M3U URL and assign region filters (UK, US, LatAm, MENA) for faster navigation.

Advantages: Supports multi-audio switching, trailers inside VOD, and remote-friendly grid view ideal for sports bars and hospitality clients.

Probleemoplossing

No channels after upload? Verify you pasted the M3U URL correctly, disable MAC filtering on your router, and ensure the TV is hardwired or on 5 GHz Wi-Fi.

Stretched aspect ratio? In Smart IPTV, set Aspect Ratio ▸ Screen Fit or use the remote’s P.Size button on Samsung to cycle through modes.

App removed from Samsung store? Side-load the Tizen package via USB (download from siptv.app) or direct customers to DuplexPlay / Net IPTV.
