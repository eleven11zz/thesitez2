Install TVMaster on Amazon Fire TV & Firestick

Side-load IPTV Smarters or TiviMate on any Fire TV device, connect your TVMaster playlist, and unlock 30K+ live channels, 80K+ VOD titles, and PPV events in crisp 4K. This walkthrough covers Downloader sideloading, playback optimisation, and common fixes so you can launch in under 10 minutes.

Works worldwide on Fire TV, Firestick Lite, Cube, and Omni TVs Setup time: 8 minutes · Skill: Beginner Requirements: Amazon account, Downloader app, TVMaster M3U & EPG URLs

Step-by-step installation

  1. Prepare your Fire TV – Power on, connect to a 25 Mbps+ network, sign into your Amazon account, and install the Downloader app from the Amazon Appstore.
  2. Enable sideloading – Go to Settings ▸ My Fire TV ▸ Developer Options, toggle Install Unknown Apps, and set Downloader to ON.
  3. Download IPTV Smarters Pro – Launch Downloader, enter https://bit.ly/smarters-fire, press Go, then install the APK when prompted.
  4. Log into TVMaster – Open IPTV Smarters, choose Load Your Playlist or File/URL, select Xtream Codes API, and paste the TVMaster portal URL, username, and password from your welcome email.
  5. Sync EPG & VOD – After login, press Refresh Channels and Update EPG. Enable Auto Update so new sports fixtures sync nightly.
  6. Optimise playback – Under Settings ▸ Player Selection, choose Hardware Decoder for 4K streams. Turn on Master Search for rapid channel and movie lookup.

Post-setup essentials

Create profiles for housemates: IPTV Smarters supports up to 4 profiles. Segment sports, kids, and international content so each viewer has tailored favourites.

Boost match-day reliability: Connect via Ethernet adapter or Wi-Fi 6 router, disable ISP parental filters, and run the built-in network speed test before kickoff.

Record replays with TiviMate: Prefer advanced DVR? Install TiviMate (Premium) via https://bit.ly/tivimate-apk, add the same credentials, and schedule recordings for overnight events.

Troubleshooting & quick fixes

Buffering on 4K streams? Drop resolution to 1080p for congested primetime windows, toggle to System Player, or clear cache under Settings ▸ Applications.

Playlist expired message? Verify your subscription status in the TVMaster dashboard, regenerate credentials if needed, and re-enter the new portal URL.

No audio on certain channels? Switch audio track via the on-screen player controls or set default to System Default under player settings.
