1. Introduction to IPTV Extreme

What is IPTV Extreme?

IPTV Extreme is a powerful and feature-rich IPTV player application designed for Android devices. It allows you to watch live TV channels, movies, TV series, and VOD content using your IPTV subscription. The app is known for its user-friendly interface, extensive format support, and advanced features like recording and catch-up TV.

Note: IPTV Extreme is a player application only. You need an active IPTV subscription (like TVMaster VIP) to watch content.

Key Features

Live TV & VOD

Watch live channels, movies, and TV series with smooth playback

EPG Support

Electronic Program Guide with full TV schedules

Catch-up TV

Watch missed programs from the past 7 days

Recording

Record your favorite shows and movies

Favorites

Create custom lists of your favorite channels

Parental Control

Lock channels and content with PIN protection

Compatible Devices

  • Android Phones & Tablets - Android 5.0 and above
  • Android TV Boxes - All Android TV devices
  • Amazon Fire TV Stick - All Fire TV models
  • Smart TVs - Android TV powered televisions

2. Installation Guide

2.1

Android Phone/Tablet Installation

  1. Open Google Play Store

    Tap the Play Store icon on your Android device.

  2. Search for IPTV Extreme

    Type "IPTV Extreme" in the search bar and press search.

  3. Select the App

    Look for "IPTV Extreme Pro" by Paolo Federico (or the free version "IPTV Extreme").

  4. Install

    Tap the "Install" button and wait for the download to complete.

  5. Open the App

    Once installed, tap "Open" to launch IPTV Extreme.

2.2

Amazon Fire TV Stick Installation

  1. Enable Apps from Unknown Sources

    Go to Settings → My Fire TV → Developer Options → Install Unknown Apps → Turn on for your browser.

  2. Download Downloader App

    From Amazon Appstore, search and install "Downloader" by AFTVnews.com.

  3. Open Downloader

    Launch the Downloader app and accept permissions.

  4. Enter URL

    In the URL field, enter: https://www.iptvextreme.eu/ and download the APK.

  5. Install APK

    Once downloaded, click "Install" and wait for installation to complete.

  6. Launch App

    You can now find IPTV Extreme in your Fire TV apps section.

Alternative: You can also sideload IPTV Extreme using Apps2Fire or similar tools from your Android phone.
2.3

Android TV Installation

  1. Open Google Play Store

    Navigate to the Play Store on your Android TV.

  2. Search for IPTV Extreme

    Use the search function or voice search to find "IPTV Extreme".

  3. Install

    Select the app and click "Install".

  4. Launch

    Open the app from your Android TV home screen or app drawer.

3. First Launch & Initial Setup

When you first open IPTV Extreme, you'll be greeted with a setup wizard to help you get started.

Initial Configuration Steps

  1. Welcome Screen

    Read the welcome message and tap "Next" or "Get Started".

  2. Grant Permissions

    The app may request storage permissions for recording and EPG data. Tap "Allow" to grant access.

  3. Select Language

    Choose your preferred language from the list. You can change this later in settings.

  4. Choose Player

    IPTV Extreme offers built-in player or external player support. For best experience, select "Internal Player".

  5. Ready to Add Playlist

    You'll now see the main screen with an option to add your IPTV playlist.

Tip: The setup wizard only appears on first launch. All settings can be changed later from the app's settings menu.

4. Adding Your IPTV Playlist

IPTV Extreme supports two main methods for adding playlists: M3U URL and Xtream Codes API. Use the credentials provided by your TVMaster VIP subscription.

4.1

Method 1: Add M3U URL Playlist

  1. Open Playlist Menu

    Tap the menu icon (☰) in the top-left corner, then select "Playlists".

  2. Add New Playlist

    Tap the "+" button or "Add Playlist" option.

  3. Select "Remote Playlist (URL)"

    Choose the remote playlist option to add an M3U URL.

  4. Enter Playlist Details

    Fill in the following information:

    Playlist Name: TVMaster VIP Playlist URL: http://your-server.com:port/get.php?username=your_username&password=your_password&type=m3u_plus&output=ts Replace with your actual credentials from TVMaster VIP
  5. Save and Load

    Tap "Save" or "OK". The app will download and parse your playlist. This may take 30-60 seconds.

  6. Verify Channels

    Once loaded, you should see all your channels categorized in the channel list.

4.2

Method 2: Add Xtream Codes API

  1. Open Playlist Menu

    Tap the menu icon (☰) and select "Playlists".

  2. Add New Playlist

    Tap the "+" button or "Add Playlist".

  3. Select "Xtream Codes"

    Choose the Xtream Codes API option (recommended for best performance).

  4. Enter API Credentials

    Fill in your TVMaster VIP credentials:

    Playlist Name: TVMaster VIP Server URL: http://your-server.com:port Username: your_username Password: your_password Use credentials provided in your TVMaster VIP welcome email
  5. Save and Connect

    Tap "Save" or "Connect". The app will authenticate and load your content.

  6. Browse Content

    You'll now have access to Live TV, Movies, and Series sections.

Recommendation: Xtream Codes API is preferred as it provides better organization, faster loading, and access to VOD categories, series with episode lists, and catch-up TV features.

5. Interface Overview

Main Menu

The main menu is accessed via the hamburger icon (☰) in the top-left corner:

  • Live TV - Browse and watch live channels
  • Movies - Access VOD movie library (Xtream Codes only)
  • Series - Browse TV shows and episodes (Xtream Codes only)
  • Playlists - Manage your IPTV playlists
  • Favorites - Quick access to your favorite channels
  • Recordings - View and manage recorded content
  • EPG - Electronic Program Guide
  • Settings - Configure app preferences

Channel List View

The channel list displays all available channels organized by category:

  • Categories - Channels grouped by type (Sports, Movies, News, etc.)
  • Search - Tap the search icon to quickly find channels
  • Grid/List View - Switch between grid and list layouts
  • Channel Logos - Visual identification with channel logos
  • Now Playing Info - Current program displayed under channel name

Video Player Controls

When watching a channel, you have access to these controls:

  • Play/Pause - Tap screen center or use play button
  • Volume - Use device volume buttons or on-screen slider
  • Channel Info - Displays current program name and schedule
  • Channel Switching - Swipe up/down or use arrow buttons to change channels
  • Aspect Ratio - Adjust video fit (original, stretch, zoom, crop)
  • Audio Track - Select alternative audio tracks if available
  • Subtitles - Enable/disable subtitles if provided in stream

EPG Integration

Access program guide information while watching:

  • Info Button - Tap to see full program details and schedule
  • Timeline View - Horizontal timeline showing past and upcoming programs
  • Catch-up Access - Tap past programs to watch catch-up (if supported)
  • Reminders - Set reminders for upcoming programs
Remote Control Support: IPTV Extreme fully supports Android TV remotes, Fire TV remotes, and external USB/Bluetooth controllers for navigation.

6. EPG (Electronic Program Guide) Setup

EPG provides TV listings showing what's currently playing and upcoming programs. IPTV Extreme supports automatic EPG from Xtream Codes or manual EPG URLs.

6.1

Automatic EPG (Xtream Codes)

If you're using Xtream Codes API, EPG is automatically included:

  1. EPG Auto-loads

    EPG data is downloaded automatically when you add a Xtream Codes playlist.

  2. Auto-Update

    EPG refreshes automatically every 24 hours.

  3. Verify EPG

    Open any channel - you should see current program name and time below channel logo.

6.2

Manual EPG Setup (M3U Playlists)

For M3U playlists, you may need to add EPG URL manually:

  1. Get EPG URL

    Obtain the EPG URL from your TVMaster VIP account or welcome email.

  2. Open Settings

    Go to Menu (☰) → Settings → EPG.

  3. Add EPG Source

    Tap "Add EPG Source" or "EPG URL".

  4. Enter URL
    EPG URL: http://your-server.com:port/xmltv.php?username=your_username&password=your_password Replace with your TVMaster VIP credentials
  5. Download EPG

    Tap "Download EPG" or "Update EPG". First download may take 2-5 minutes.

  6. Enable Auto-Update

    Turn on "Auto-update EPG" to refresh daily at a specified time.

EPG Update Schedule: Set EPG auto-update during low-usage hours (e.g., 3:00 AM) to avoid interrupting viewing. EPG data is typically 5-10 MB per day.

7. Advanced Features

Managing Favorites

Create custom lists of your most-watched channels:

  1. Add to Favorites

    Long-press any channel in the list and select "Add to Favorites" or tap the star icon.

  2. Access Favorites

    Go to Menu (☰) → Favorites to see all your favorite channels in one place.

  3. Remove from Favorites

    Long-press a favorite channel and select "Remove from Favorites".

  4. Reorder Favorites

    Long-press and drag to reorder channels in your favorites list.

Parental Control

Lock specific channels or categories to restrict access:

  1. Enable Parental Control

    Go to Settings → Parental Control and toggle it on.

  2. Set PIN Code

    Create a 4-digit PIN code. Remember this - it's required to unlock content.

  3. Lock Channels/Categories

    Long-press any channel or category and select "Lock". Locked content shows a padlock icon.

  4. Unlock Content

    Attempting to access locked content prompts for PIN entry.

Catch-up TV

Watch programs from the past 7 days (requires Xtream Codes and provider support):

  1. Open EPG

    While watching a channel, tap the Info button to open EPG.

  2. Select Past Program

    Browse the timeline and tap any program from the past 7 days.

  3. Start Playback

    The program will load and start playing from the beginning.

  4. Player Controls

    Use pause, play, and seek controls just like VOD content.

Note: Catch-up availability depends on your IPTV provider. TVMaster VIP offers catch-up on most major channels.

Recording Programs

Record live TV to watch later (storage permission required):

  1. Start Recording

    While watching live TV, tap the record button (red circle icon).

  2. Choose Duration

    Select recording duration or choose "Until manually stopped".

  3. Recording Active

    A notification appears confirming recording. You can minimize and use other apps.

  4. Access Recordings

    Go to Menu (☰) → Recordings to view all saved content.

  5. Manage Storage

    Recordings are saved to internal storage. Delete old recordings to free up space.

Search Functionality

Quickly find channels, movies, or series:

  1. Open Search

    Tap the search icon (magnifying glass) in the top toolbar.

  2. Enter Query

    Type channel name, movie title, or series name.

  3. Filter Results

    Results show across all content types (Live TV, Movies, Series).

  4. Quick Access

    Tap any result to immediately start playback.

Customize IPTV Extreme to your preferences through comprehensive settings options:

9. Troubleshooting Common Issues

Channels Not Loading / Buffering

Causes:

  • Slow internet connection
  • Server overload
  • Incorrect buffer settings

Solutions:

  • Check internet speed (minimum 10 Mbps recommended)
  • Increase buffer size: Settings → Player → Buffer Size → 10 seconds
  • Enable hardware acceleration
  • Switch to lower quality stream if available
  • Restart router and device

Black Screen / No Video

Causes:

  • Codec incompatibility
  • Hardware acceleration issues
  • Corrupt stream

Solutions:

  • Go to Settings → Player → Video Decoder → Switch to Software
  • Toggle hardware acceleration off/on
  • Try different channel to verify if issue is stream-specific
  • Update IPTV Extreme to latest version
  • Clear app cache: Settings → Storage → Clear Cache

No Audio / Audio Sync Issues

Causes:

  • Audio codec not supported
  • Audio delay settings
  • Device audio output issue

Solutions:

  • Check device volume and ensure not muted
  • Settings → Player → Audio Output → Try different option
  • Adjust audio delay: Long-press during playback → Audio Delay
  • Switch audio track if multiple available
  • Restart the app

EPG Not Showing / Not Updating

Causes:

  • EPG URL not configured
  • EPG download failed
  • Auto-update disabled

Solutions:

  • Verify EPG URL in Settings → EPG
  • Manually trigger EPG update: Settings → EPG → Update Now
  • Enable auto-update: Settings → EPG → Auto-update EPG
  • Check storage space (EPG requires 5-10 MB)
  • Re-add playlist if using Xtream Codes

Playlist Not Loading

Causes:

  • Incorrect URL or credentials
  • Server down or maintenance
  • Subscription expired

Solutions:

  • Double-check credentials match your TVMaster VIP account
  • Verify URL format (include http:// or https://)
  • Test credentials in browser or another device
  • Check subscription status in TVMaster VIP dashboard
  • Contact TVMaster VIP support if issue persists

App Crashes / Freezes

Causes:

  • Insufficient device memory
  • App data corruption
  • Outdated app version

Solutions:

  • Clear app cache and data: Device Settings → Apps → IPTV Extreme
  • Close background apps to free up RAM
  • Update to latest IPTV Extreme version
  • Restart device
  • Reinstall app if problems continue

10. Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use IPTV Extreme on multiple devices?

Yes! You can install IPTV Extreme on unlimited devices. However, simultaneous streaming is limited by your TVMaster VIP subscription plan. Most plans allow 1-3 concurrent connections. Check your subscription details for specific limits.

Is IPTV Extreme free or paid?

IPTV Extreme offers both a free version with ads and a Pro version ($5.99 one-time purchase) without ads and with additional features like custom themes and advanced recording options. Both versions work with your TVMaster VIP subscription.

How much internet speed do I need?

Recommended speeds: SD quality (480p): 5 Mbps | HD quality (720p): 10 Mbps | Full HD (1080p): 20 Mbps | 4K Ultra HD: 50 Mbps. These are per stream, so multiple devices require proportionally more bandwidth.

Why do some channels buffer more than others?

Different channels have different bitrates (video quality). Sports and movie channels in HD/4K require more bandwidth. Peak viewing times can also cause congestion. Try increasing buffer size or watching during off-peak hours for smoother playback.

Can I use VPN with IPTV Extreme?

Yes, IPTV Extreme works with VPN services. A VPN can improve privacy and may help access geo-restricted content. However, VPNs can sometimes slow down streaming. Choose a fast VPN server close to your location or the IPTV server location for best results.

How do I backup my settings and favorites?

Go to Settings → Backup & Restore → Create Backup. This saves your playlists, favorites, EPG settings, and app preferences to a file. You can restore this backup on another device or after reinstalling the app.

What's the difference between M3U and Xtream Codes?

M3U: Simple playlist format, single URL, all content in one list. Xtream Codes: Advanced API with better organization, separate sections for Live TV/Movies/Series, faster loading, automatic EPG, catch-up support. We recommend Xtream Codes for best experience.

Where are recordings saved and how much space do they use?

Recordings are saved to your device's internal storage by default (you can change this in Settings → Storage → Recording Location). Space usage depends on quality and duration: SD quality uses ~1 GB/hour, HD uses ~2-3 GB/hour, Full HD uses ~4-5 GB/hour.
