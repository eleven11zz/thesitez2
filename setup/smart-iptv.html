1. Introduction to Smart IPTV

What is Smart IPTV?

Smart IPTV (SIPTV) is one of the most popular IPTV applications for Samsung and LG Smart TVs. It allows you to watch live TV channels using your own IPTV subscription through a simple, TV-remote-friendly interface. Smart IPTV is known for its reliability, ease of use, and excellent compatibility with major Smart TV brands.

Important: Smart IPTV requires a one-time activation fee of €5.49 (approximately $5.99 USD) for 1 year. You must activate your device's MAC address at siptv.app before use.

Key Features

Smart TV Native

Built specifically for Samsung and LG Smart TV platforms

Remote Friendly

Optimized for TV remote control navigation

Web Management

Upload and manage playlists via web portal

EPG Support

Electronic Program Guide with TV schedules

Favorites

Create custom lists of favorite channels

Parental Control

Lock channels with PIN protection

Pricing

  • Activation Cost: €5.49 / $5.99 USD (one-time payment)
  • Subscription Duration: 1 year from activation date
  • Renewal: After 1 year, reactivation required at same price
  • Payment Methods: Credit card, PayPal, cryptocurrency
  • No Hidden Fees: No monthly subscription, one payment per year

3. Activation Process

Before you can use Smart IPTV, you must activate your TV's MAC address on the Smart IPTV website. This is a mandatory step.

3.1

Find Your TV's MAC Address

For Samsung Smart TV:

  1. Press Menu/Settings

    Use your remote to open the Settings menu.

  2. Navigate to Support

    Go to Support → Contact Samsung (or About This TV).

  3. Find MAC Address

    Look for "MAC Address (Wireless)" or "MAC Address (Wired)". Write this down - it looks like: AA:BB:CC:DD:EE:FF

For LG Smart TV:

  1. Press Settings/Gear Icon

    Open the Settings menu on your LG TV.

  2. Navigate to Network

    Go to Network → Wi-Fi Connection (or Wired Connection).

  3. View Network Information

    Select "Advanced Wi-Fi Settings" or "Edit". The MAC address is displayed at the bottom.

Alternative Method: Install Smart IPTV first (see Installation section), launch the app, and it will display your MAC address on the welcome screen.
3.2

Activate on Smart IPTV Website

  1. Visit Smart IPTV Portal

    Open a web browser and go to: https://siptv.app

  2. Click "Activation"

    On the homepage, click the "Activation" button in the top menu.

  3. Enter MAC Address
    MAC Address: AA:BB:CC:DD:EE:FF Enter your TV's MAC address without spaces or dashes
  4. Enter Email

    Provide a valid email address - you'll receive activation confirmation here.

  5. Complete Payment

    Choose payment method (credit card, PayPal, or crypto) and pay €5.49.

  6. Wait for Activation

    Activation is usually instant but can take up to 10-15 minutes. You'll receive a confirmation email.

Activation Duration: Your activation is valid for exactly 1 year from the payment date. Mark your calendar to renew before expiration.

4. Installation Guide

4.1

Method 1: Install from App Store (If Available)

Samsung Smart TV:

  1. Open Samsung Apps

    Press the Smart Hub button on your remote, then select "Apps".

  2. Search for Smart IPTV

    Use the search icon and type "Smart IPTV" or "SIPTV".

  3. Install

    Select the app and click "Install" or "Download".

  4. Launch

    Once installed, open Smart IPTV from your Apps list.

LG Smart TV:

  1. Open LG Content Store

    Press the Home button and select "LG Content Store".

  2. Search for Smart IPTV

    Navigate to Apps & Games, use search to find "Smart IPTV".

  3. Install

    Click "Install" and wait for download to complete.

  4. Launch

    Open Smart IPTV from your app launcher.

4.2

Method 2: Install via USB (If Not in Store)

If Smart IPTV is not available in your region's app store, you can install it manually:

Samsung Tizen TVs:

  1. Download SIPTV Widget

    Visit the Smart IPTV website download section and get the Tizen .wgt file.

  2. Prepare USB Drive

    Format USB drive to FAT32. Create a folder named "userwidget" in the root.

  3. Copy Widget File

    Place the downloaded .wgt file inside the "userwidget" folder.

  4. Insert USB

    Turn off your TV completely. Insert USB drive into TV's USB port.

  5. Turn On TV

    Power on the TV. The app will auto-install (may take 30-60 seconds).

  6. Find App

    Go to Apps → Downloaded Apps to find Smart IPTV.

LG webOS TVs:

  1. Enable Developer Mode

    Press Home 3 times, then enter "Dev Mode" in the launcher bar.

  2. Download webOS App

    Get the Smart IPTV webOS package (.ipk file) from the website.

  3. Use LG Developer Tools

    Install LG webOS TV SDK on your computer and use CLI tools to sideload the app.

Caution: Sideloading requires technical knowledge. If unsure, contact Smart IPTV support or TVMaster VIP for assistance.

5. Uploading Your Playlist

Smart IPTV requires you to upload your M3U playlist to their web portal. The playlist is then synced to your TV automatically.

5.1

Upload via Web Portal

  1. Visit Smart IPTV Portal

    Go to https://siptv.app and log in (or continue as guest).

  2. Click "Upload Playlist"

    Select "My List" → "Upload Playlist" from the top menu.

  3. Enter MAC Address

    Enter your TV's MAC address that you activated earlier.

  4. Choose Upload Method

    You have three options:

    • Upload M3U File - Upload an M3U file from your computer
    • Enter M3U URL - Paste your TVMaster VIP M3U URL
    • Manually Add Channels - Add channels one by one (not recommended for large lists)
  5. Using M3U URL (Recommended)

    Get your M3U URL from TVMaster VIP and paste it:

    M3U URL Example: http://your-server.com:port/get.php?username=your_username&password=your_password&type=m3u_plus&output=ts Replace with your actual TVMaster VIP credentials
  6. Add EPG URL (Optional)

    For TV guide functionality, add your EPG URL:

    EPG URL Example: http://your-server.com:port/xmltv.php?username=your_username&password=your_password EPG provides program schedules and descriptions
  7. Save Playlist

    Click "Save" or "Upload". Processing may take 1-5 minutes depending on playlist size.

  8. Restart Smart IPTV on TV

    Close and reopen the Smart IPTV app on your TV to sync the new playlist.

Auto-Update: When you upload a playlist URL (not file), Smart IPTV automatically updates your channels every 24-48 hours. No need to manually re-upload.
5.2

Managing Your Playlist

You can edit your playlist anytime via the web portal:

  • Reorder Channels - Drag and drop channels to change order
  • Delete Channels - Remove unwanted channels from your list
  • Add Custom Channels - Manually add individual streams
  • Create Groups/Categories - Organize channels into custom categories
  • Update URLs - Change M3U or EPG URLs when credentials change
  • Multiple Playlists - Store multiple playlists and switch between them

All changes sync automatically to your TV within minutes.

6. Interface Overview

Main Screen

Smart IPTV has a simple, TV-remote-friendly interface:

  • Channel List (Left Panel) - All your channels organized by category
  • Video Player (Center) - Live TV playback area
  • Program Info (Bottom) - Current program name and EPG details
  • Mini EPG (Optional) - Timeline view of current and upcoming programs

Remote Control Navigation

Smart IPTV is designed for TV remote controls:

  • Arrow Up/Down - Navigate channel list
  • Arrow Left/Right - Browse EPG timeline or switch categories
  • OK/Enter - Select channel to play
  • Back - Return to channel list or exit player
  • Number Keys - Direct channel entry (if enabled in settings)
  • Color Buttons - Quick shortcuts (customizable)
  • Menu/Settings - Open app settings menu

Channel Information

While watching a channel:

  • Press Info/i Button - View current program details
  • Press OK - Show full EPG for the channel
  • Press Up/Down - Quick channel change
  • Long Press OK - Add channel to favorites

Category View

Channels are organized into categories (Sports, Movies, News, etc.):

  • Press Left - Switch to category list view
  • Select Category - Filter channels by selected category
  • "All Channels" - View complete unsorted channel list
  • "Favorites" - Quick access to starred channels

7. Advanced Features

Favorites Management

  1. Add to Favorites

    While watching a channel, long-press OK button or select "Add to Favorites" from menu.

  2. Access Favorites

    Press Left to open categories, then select "Favorites" at the top of the list.

  3. Remove from Favorites

    While on a favorited channel, long-press OK again to remove.

  4. Reorder Favorites

    Use the web portal to drag-and-drop favorite channels in custom order.

Parental Control

  1. Enable Parental Control

    Go to Settings (Menu button) → Parental Control → Toggle ON.

  2. Set PIN Code

    Create a 4-digit PIN. Remember this code - you'll need it to access locked channels.

  3. Lock Channels

    Long-press on any channel, select "Lock Channel". A padlock icon appears.

  4. Lock Categories

    In category view, long-press a category to lock all channels within it (e.g., Adult content).

  5. Unlock Content

    When accessing locked content, enter your PIN to temporarily unlock (session-based).

EPG Program Guide

If you've configured an EPG URL, you can access rich TV guide features:

  • Full EPG View - Press OK on a channel to see full 7-day program schedule
  • Program Descriptions - Select any program to read synopsis and details
  • Timeline Navigation - Press Left/Right to browse past and future programs
  • Channel Logos - EPG displays channel logos for visual identification
  • Auto-Update - EPG refreshes daily automatically

Search Function

Quickly find channels by name:

  1. Open Search

    Press the Search button on your remote (or select Search from menu).

  2. Enter Channel Name

    Use on-screen keyboard to type channel name (e.g., "ESPN", "CNN").

  3. View Results

    Matching channels appear in real-time as you type.

  4. Select and Play

    Choose a channel from results to start playback immediately.

Auto-Startup

Configure Smart IPTV to launch automatically when TV turns on:

  1. Go to Settings → General → Auto-Start
  2. Enable "Launch on TV startup"
  3. Optionally enable "Auto-play last channel" to resume where you left off

9. Troubleshooting Common Issues

"Not Activated" Message

Causes:

  • Device not activated on siptv.app
  • Activation expired (after 1 year)
  • Incorrect MAC address used

Solutions:

  • Verify your MAC address in Settings → System Info
  • Check activation status at siptv.app
  • Reactivate if expired (€5.49 for another year)
  • Wait 10-15 minutes after payment for activation to complete
  • Restart Smart IPTV app after activating

Empty Channel List / No Channels

Causes:

  • Playlist not uploaded to web portal
  • Incorrect MAC address used during upload
  • Playlist URL expired or invalid

Solutions:

  • Go to siptv.app → My List → Upload Playlist
  • Verify you're using correct MAC address (check in app settings)
  • Test your M3U URL in VLC player first to confirm it works
  • Wait 2-5 minutes after upload, then restart app
  • Try uploading file instead of URL if URL method fails

Channels Buffering / Freezing

Causes:

  • Slow internet connection
  • Wi-Fi signal weak
  • Server overload
  • Buffer settings too low

Solutions:

  • Test internet speed (minimum 10 Mbps for SD, 20 Mbps for HD)
  • Switch to wired Ethernet connection instead of Wi-Fi
  • Increase buffer size: Settings → Player → Buffer Size → High
  • Lower video quality: Settings → Player → Resolution → SD
  • Restart router and TV
  • Try different channels to isolate if issue is specific stream

Black Screen / Channel Won't Play

Causes:

  • Stream offline or dead link
  • Codec incompatibility
  • Connection timeout

Solutions:

  • Try different channel - if only one channel affected, stream may be down
  • Wait 30-60 seconds for stream to load
  • Enable hardware acceleration: Settings → Player → Hardware Acceleration
  • Increase timeout: Settings → Network → Timeout → 30 seconds
  • Update playlist at siptv.app if many channels not working

EPG Not Showing / Not Updating

Causes:

  • EPG URL not configured
  • EPG URL invalid or expired
  • Channel IDs don't match EPG data

Solutions:

  • Verify EPG URL at siptv.app → My List → Edit
  • Test EPG URL in browser - should download XML file
  • Re-upload playlist with correct EPG URL
  • Clear cache: Settings → General → Clear Cache
  • Wait 24 hours for EPG to fully populate
  • Contact TVMaster VIP support if EPG URL not working

App Crashes / Closes Unexpectedly

Causes:

  • Insufficient TV memory
  • Corrupted app data
  • TV firmware outdated

Solutions:

  • Close other running apps to free up memory
  • Restart your Smart TV (full power cycle)
  • Clear Smart IPTV cache: Settings → Clear Cache
  • Update TV firmware: TV Settings → Support → Software Update
  • Reinstall Smart IPTV app
  • Contact Smart IPTV support if crashes persist

10. Frequently Asked Questions

How long is Smart IPTV activation valid?

Smart IPTV activation is valid for exactly 1 year from the date of payment. After 1 year, you must reactivate your device at siptv.app for another €5.49. You'll receive an email reminder before expiration.

Can I use one activation on multiple TVs?

No. Each TV has a unique MAC address and requires its own activation. If you have 3 Smart TVs, you need 3 separate activations (€5.49 each). However, you can use the same playlist on all devices.

What happens if I don't renew after 1 year?

The Smart IPTV app will stop working and display "Not Activated" message. Your playlist settings are saved on the web portal, so when you reactivate, everything will work again without re-uploading your playlist.

How many channels can I add to Smart IPTV?

There's no official limit, but for best performance, we recommend keeping your playlist under 2,000 channels. Larger playlists may cause slower loading and navigation. TVMaster VIP offers optimized playlists with 500-1,500 channels.

Can I use Xtream Codes API with Smart IPTV?

No, Smart IPTV only supports M3U playlists and M3U URLs. You cannot directly use Xtream Codes API. However, you can convert Xtream Codes to M3U URL format - TVMaster VIP provides both formats for compatibility.

Does Smart IPTV support VOD (Movies & Series)?

Yes, but with limitations. Smart IPTV can display VOD content if included in your M3U playlist, but it doesn't have a dedicated VOD section like other apps. All content appears in the channel list. For better VOD experience, consider using IPTV Smarters or SMART ONE IPTV.

Is Smart IPTV legal?

Yes, Smart IPTV is a legal application. It's simply a media player - legality depends on your content source. TVMaster VIP is a legitimate IPTV service provider. Always ensure you're using licensed content from authorized providers.

Can I share my Smart IPTV activation with friends/family?

No. Each activation is tied to a specific MAC address (TV device). However, you CAN share your IPTV playlist/credentials - multiple people can upload the same TVMaster VIP M3U URL to their own Smart IPTV activations (subject to TVMaster VIP's connection limits).
