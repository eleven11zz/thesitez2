Big game FAQ

Is 4K available outside the U.S.?

Yes. Sky Sports offers a native UHD broadcast in the U.K., while DAZN Canada carries the NFL Game Pass 4K stream. TVMaster mirrors both feeds; pick the UHD channel in your app.

Can I pause or rewind the live broadcast?

Enable timeshift in TiviMate, IPTV Smarters, or Formuler. Allocate 15–20 GB to buffer four hours of coverage, including pre-game and trophy presentation.

What about halftime audio sync?

If vocals drift during the halftime show, switch your decoder to System or Hardware depending on your device, or manually adjust audio delay (+/- 200 ms) in the player settings.
