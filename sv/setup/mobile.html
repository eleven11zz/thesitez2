Stream TVMaster on iPhone, iPad & Android

Keep every Premier League match, US network, and Asian variety show in your pocket. This tutorial covers installing IPTV Smarters (iOS/Android), Televizo, and GSE Smart IPTV, including push notifications, Chromecast/AirPlay casting, and mobile data optimisation.

Enheter: iPhone, iPad, Android phones & tablets (Android 7+) Installation time: 5 minuter · Skill: Beginner Requirements: TVMaster credentials, stable Wi-Fi or 4G/5G connection

Why promote mobile streaming

  • Viewers catch live fixtures on the go—boosting trial-to-paid conversions during commutes and travel.
  • App notifications alert users when F1 qualifying, Champions League kickoffs, or UFC cards go live.
  • Chromecast and AirPlay mirror instantly to Smarta TV-apparater for hotel or friends’ house streaming.
  • Offline VOD downloads (where available) keep kids entertained without consuming mobile data.

Installera IPTV Smarters (iOS & Android)

  1. Ladda ner the app – Search IPTV Smarters in the App Store or Google Play and install.
  2. Accept permissions – Allow notifications for match alerts and enable local storage to download logos and EPG.
  3. Add TVMaster account – Choose Load Your Playlist or File/URL ▸ Xtream Codes API, then paste the TVMaster portal URL, username, and password.
  4. Sync channels – Tap Allow to update channels, movies, and series. Wait for the 30K+ channel database to build.
  5. Create favourites – Star your go-to leagues (EPL, NBA, Thai dramas, etc.) for faster access under Favourites.
  6. Enable casting – Open any channel, tap the Cast or AirPlay icon, and select your TV or Chromecast for big-screen streaming.

Alternative: Televizo (Android)

Playlist add-on: In Televizo, choose Playlist ▸ Login / password, enter TVMaster credentials, and enable auto-sync.

Data saver: Toggle Adaptive stream and set mobile data quality to 720p for smooth performance on 4G.

Interface: Use TV-guides ▸ Layout ▸ Compact so hundreds of channels display cleanly on phones.

Mobile troubleshooting

Buffering on mobile data? Lower the player buffer to 6 seconds, disable VPNs, and ensure 4G signal > -95 dBm. Recommend 5G or Wi-Fi for 4K feeds.

Playlist expired? Go to Användare ▸ Edit ▸ Refresh in IPTV Smarters, or remove/re-add the account after renewing.

No audio? On iOS, switch to a different decoder; on Android, toggle Audio Track ▸ Stereo.
